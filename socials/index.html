Socials

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and Golang UK is no exception. We'll be arranging three social events - before, during and after the conference, with something for everyone.

Locations and more info will be announced soon.

Wednesday

August 17

Wednesday night kicks off the conference with a bang. We'll meet at Bar Kick in Shoreditch from 6pm for beverages and awesome table football action (we'll be in the basement bar). Let us know if you'll be making it over @GolangUKConf. The Wednesday social is open to anyone.
