Socials

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and Golang UK is no exception. We'll be arranging two social events - one before and one during the conference, with something for everyone.

Locations and more info will be announced soon.

Pre-conference Social

Wednesday August 17

Wednesday night kicks off the conference at Bar Kick in Shoreditch. Starting at 6pm, there will be a cash bar and some awesome free table football action. We'll be in the basement bar. The Wednesday social is open to anyone with and interest in Go.

Bar Kick
127 Shoreditch High St, London E1 6JE
020 7739 8700



Main Conference Social

Thursday August 18

Head outside to the Brewery courtyard right after talks finish and join us in the marquee for some dinner and drinks while networking with fellow gophers.

On the menu is a Brazilian BBQ buffet, including vegetarian and vegan options. There will be a few free drinks and a cash bar to follow.

Please note: our Code of Conduct applies to all venues associated with the conference, including off-site social events.
