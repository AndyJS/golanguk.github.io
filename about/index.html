Organisers

Johanna Cherry is a software engineer at CrowdSurge in London working on their APIs and services. She has over 11 years experience developing web applications with PHP and lots of other cool web technologies. Prior to CrowdSurge, she was Data Team Lead at Hailo and Development Manager for the affiliate network Webgains and Technical Team Lead for the PHP consultancy Ibuildings. She earned a Bachelor of Science in Technology Management from Herzing University in Madison Wisconsin.

Johanna manages speakers, socials, the venue, marketing, and delegate services for the conference.

Sam Bell is co-founder of charting startup ChartBlocks, an online chart building tool. He is also managing director of web agency Copia Digital where he and his team build websites and web applications for hedge funds and asset managers, helping them report on fund performance to their investors.

Sam manages registration, sponsors, exhibits, and volunteers for the conference.

David Hernandez is an independent Software Engineer in London, he helps companies to improve their software. He worked different countries like Spain, UK and Australia, participating in projects like the BBC London Olympics 2012, helping to achieve Continuous Delivery at Atlassian, or delivering services to the citizens at the Government Digital Services.

You can find David speaking and collaborating at Go London User Group, because Go is his favourite language.
