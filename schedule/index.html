Schedule

The Golang UK Conference 2016 is two days packed with interesting talks from some of the most well known speakers in the world. The first day is for tutorial ticket holders while the second day will include two simultaneous tracks of talks so that you are certain to find something of interest at every point throughout the conference.

The schedule will be announced in mid 2016. Until then you can take check out videos from last year's event or you can submit to our Call for Papers here.
